About

A little about me

"जैसे मोरपंख में हर रंग अपनी कहानी कहता है, वैसे ही हर डिज़ाइन में मेरी आत्मा बसती है।"

I'm Aayushi Keer, a graphic designer who finds inspiration in the timeless aesthetics of Lord Krishna — the divine play of color, rhythm and emotion. My work blends traditional Indian motifs with modern visual language to create designs that feel both sacred and contemporary.

From brand identities to social campaigns, illustrations to print collateral — I create visuals that resonate.
